"Hello! Sharing Dishes!"

EXPERIENCE THE BEST 4 CHEFS
NEW BRANCH IN CAIRO
2023

Indulge in a special tasting menu, meet and greet with the chefs, witness a cooking competition, and enjoy a wine pairing dinner featuring dishes created by the best 4 chefs

2023.10.15(THU) ~ 16(FRI)

@ National Museum of Egypt, Doki cairo

Main Program

Chef's Tasting Menu
Experience a special tasting menu featuring signature dishes from each of our 4 top chefs. Each dish will be introduced by the chef who created it, giving you a chance to learn more about their culinary inspirations and techniques.
Meet the Chefs
Get to know our top 4 chefs personally and learn more about their culinary backgrounds and philosophies. This can be done as a Q&A session or as an informal mingling event.
Cooking Competition
Watch our 4 top chefs go head-to-head in a cooking competition to create the best dish using a specific ingredient or theme. You'll be able to taste the dishes and a winner will be chosen by either a panel of judges or by popular vote.
Chef's Table Experience
Indulge in a one-of-a-kind dining experience with our top 4 chefs at the Chef's Table. Enjoy an exclusive multi-course menu crafted by the chefs themselves, and get a chance to interact with them as they prepare and serve your meal.
Farm-to-Table Tour and Lunch
Embark on a guided tour of a local farm where our chefs source their fresh ingredients, and learn about the farm-to-table movement. After the tour, enjoy a farm-fresh lunch prepared by our top chefs with the ingredients you just saw being harvested.
